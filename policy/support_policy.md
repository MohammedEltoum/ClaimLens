# Support Triage Policy

Policy P1 - Product damage on arrival:
If the image shows moderate or severe physical damage that plausibly affects product use, approve a replacement when the customer requests replacement. Approve a refund when the customer requests a refund and visible damage is moderate or severe.

Policy P2 - Minor cosmetic defects:
If the image shows only minor cosmetic wear and the product appears usable, do not automatically approve a full refund. Offer troubleshooting, a partial courtesy credit, or a replacement only when the customer explicitly reports a functional issue.

Policy P3 - No visible defect:
If no defect is visible and the complaint lacks concrete evidence, deny automated refund or replacement and ask for more details or another image.

Policy P4 - Safety and escalation:
Escalate tickets involving exposed wiring, smoke, overheating, sharp broken glass, injury, legal threats, repeated failed replacements, suspected fraud, or mismatched evidence.

Policy P5 - Urgency:
High urgency should not override the evidence requirements, but high urgency combined with safety risk or severe damage should be escalated for human review.
